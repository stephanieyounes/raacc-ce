Our Services

Take a look at the services we provide from traffic services to drainage

GEOTECHNICAL & LAND DEVELOPMENT

To guaranty assurance of your investment, you must use reputable land surveying services before embarking on any new project's construction. Our highly qualified land surveyors and engineers will use cutting-edge geospatial tools to give you the essential data you need to precisely map and develop your land.

TRAFFIC ANALYSIS

Do you need to establish adequate plans for traffic both while and after your project is completed? You may rely on RAACC's traffic engineering services. Our skilled team of traffic engineers can guarantee that the completion of your new development project won't have an adverse effect on the nearby neighborhoods.

STRUCTURAL DESIGN

We are able to provide cost-effective and architecturally appealing structural designs for your project owing to our track record of completed successful projects. Experience with structural engineering includes work on parking structures, high-rise buildings, highway bridges, mildly reinforced concrete, post-tensioned concrete, precast concrete, structural steel.

DRAINAGE & UTILITIES

Our civil engineering teams have successfully completed hundreds of water distribution and wastewater collection projects across the country, giving them industry-leading knowledge in this field. In addition, utility coordination is becoming more and more crucial in today's crowded urban settings. Our utility specialists have the skills and background necessary to ensure the success of your upcoming utility project.
